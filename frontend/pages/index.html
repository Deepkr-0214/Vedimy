✦ How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

A seamless experience from account creation to your first secure exam session.

1

Create Account

Register as a student, coordinator, or teacher. Email verification keeps things secure.

2

Register Your Face

Capture 3 angles of your face. Encrypted and stored securely — no third-party access.
